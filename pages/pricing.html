Pricing & Programs FAQ.

Is there a single monthly fee, or extra retest costs?
One fee per month. Retests are discounted and capped — published before signup.
Can I switch programs mid-stream?
Yes. You can move between Evaluation, Instant Funding, and Pro tiers — fees prorate.
What's the difference between Evaluation and Instant Funding?
Evaluation is cheaper and requires you to hit a published profit target. Instant Funding skips the eval at a higher monthly fee.
What's the profit split?
80%/20% standard, 85%/15% Pro, 90%/10% Elite — locked at signup.
How fast can I get funded?
Instant Funding is same-day onboarding. Evaluation traders typically clear in 7–14 days.
Are there annual plans?
Annual prepay discounts are available — speak to a trader to set up.
